Biography

I am an aspiring data analyst.

I am currently working on independent projects to build my data analysis skills whilst working. I am interested in positions that offer a chance to grow my emerging career. Please feel free to contact me with inquiries.

In a previous life, I was a tax accountant specialising in superannuation funds and have travelled around Europe and the United Kingdom plying my trade as a bartender. Having deciding tax accountancy was not for me I enrolled at the University of Sydney to complete a Master of Economic Analysis. During my coursework and research thesis, I developed a strong interest in statistical prediction and decided to guide myself to a career in data analysis.

Proficiencies: Data analysis, data visualisation, report writing, R, Python, research, and contest theory (If the opportunity arises?).
